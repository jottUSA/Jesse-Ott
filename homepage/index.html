Let's Talk...



The idea of a dream destination has to be somewhere far, far, away, right? It's a dream having homemade pasta in Italy, spoon fed grapes on Greek Islands, or maybe going on a safari where you’re no longer at the top of the food chain. It’s all awesome but you could spend your whole life not crossing US border and never scratch the surface of what “The Land of the Free” has to offer.

… And that brings us here

My girlfriend and I have always had this anxiousness of never wanting to stay still so we try to get out as often as we can, whenever we can. Being a compulsive baseball fan, I decided to make it a goal to make the pilgrimage to every MLB park. She went along with it and hey, having to put up with a sweltering ~3 hour game for an excuse to travel is a great trade-off (although she would say otherwise).

When we go on these trips, we crowdsourced the hell out of the internet to make the most out of these long weekends (Shoutout to Thrillist.com & Eno Saris’ ____ for ideas!). I figured, if I’m going to go through the effort, I may as well jot down (hence the corny site name alliteration) the highlights of the cities were and consolidate all the go-to-places for others. This site isn’t much about us, just simply highlights of activities, must see items, and must-go items of these great cities housing venues of America’s great pastime.

Hope you enjoy these collected thoughts and scattered memories.
